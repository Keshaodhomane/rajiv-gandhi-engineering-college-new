Wecome to Rajiv Gandhi College of Engineering,
Research & Technology, Chandrapur

More than 35 years ago, it was the vision of Shri Shantaramji Potdukhe that transformed the educational profile of Chandrapur when he laid foundation of technical education by starting Rajiv Gandhi College of Engineering, Research and Technology (formerly Chandrapur Engineering College ) in 1983. It was a humble beginning with 5 branches and 180 seats. It is now has 460 intake in 7 branches ( Civil Engg, Computer Technology, Electronics)

Shri Shantaram Potdukhe
President

Rajiv Gandhi College of Engineering, Research and Technology is more than 35 years old. It is matter of pleasure coated with sense of satisfaction that it has lived up to the expectation with which it was started in 1983. It was an attempt on the part of the management to make available a centre, first of its kind, of engineering education at Chandrapur.
The engineers passing from the college and proving their technical excellence in India and abroad generate sense of pride in all of us.
